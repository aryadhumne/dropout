EduDrop is a digital platform designed to reduce primary school dropouts through early identification and structured intervention.

Our system connects Principals, Teachers, NGO Admins, and Volunteers in one centralized ecosystem. Teachers update student data, the system identifies at-risk students, and verified cases are forwarded to NGOs for timely intervention.

Instead of reacting after a student drops out, EduDrop focuses on predicting and preventing dropout before it happens.
