ABOUT

What is EduDrop?

A digital platform designed to reduce primary school dropouts through early identification and structured intervention.

1

Teachers Update Student Data

Attendance, grades, assignments, and quiz scores are entered into the system regularly by teachers for each student.

2

AI Identifies At-Risk Students

Our prediction engine analyzes patterns and flags students showing early warning signs of dropping out, with explainable reasoning.

3

Principals Review & Verify

School principals get a complete dashboard with analytics, risk trends, and can verify flagged cases before escalation.

4

NGOs Intervene & Support

Verified at-risk cases are forwarded to NGO volunteers who provide counselling, parent meetings, financial aid, and mentorship.
